Business trips and events

  • Event request form

    This form must be filled and sent to fairmat@physik.hu-berlin.de for any event such as attending a conference, visiting a research group, or organizing a FAIRmat event.

    • Download form

  • Business trip application (Dienstreiseantrag "DRA")

    This form must be filled after the internal approval of the event, and should include the anticipated costs. It is sent to HU physiks deans office for approval.

    • Download Form

  • Travel expense statement (Reisekostenabrechnung "RKA")

    This form is filled after the business trip and includes all the actual costs to be reimbursed.
